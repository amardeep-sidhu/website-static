PTC Punjabi Music Awards

PTC Punjabi music awards 2009

har saal ETC Punjabi duara karwaye jaan wale music awards, es wari PTC wallo karwaye gaye. har waar di taran ohi khaamian, kamian dekhan nu milian. sab ton changi gal jo es function wich hai k punjabi sangeet te filam jagat de sab wadde chote bande ik jagah te kathe hunde ne, fer kuch k nu sanmanit b kita janda (sahi sahi ja galat, eh ik alag swaal hai) . sirf ehna gallan karke hi eh program bahut keemat rakhda hai. gal karde a program de change maade baare. sab ton pehli gal shuraat koi bahuti changi ni c. pichli waar puraane gaanian ton lai k nave gaanian tak jaan da jihda sequence dikhaya gia bahut sohna fabbia c par es wari oh gal nazar hi nahi aai. kuch ese tarah da hi keeta janda te wadia lagna c. es wari fer galat anchor chose kite gaye. na tan shekhar suman changi taran punjabi bol sakda te na archana puran singh. masla sirf punjabi bolan tak da nahi hai, host nu punjabi music baare kuch pata b hona chahida tan ke oh program nu kuch hor sohappan de sake. gal life time achievement awards di tan Iddu Sharif, Sarabjit & Malkit nu sanmaanit karna wadia gal c. eddan de kalakaaran da sanmaan karna sada farz hai. wich wich jihdian faaltu diyan performances dikhaiyan gaiyan oh bilkul hi bakwas c. Yaana Gupta, Bombay Vikings te hindi gaanian de ki matlab hai ? sirf ese wajah karke program enne late tak chalda rehnda (main 1 waje tak dekhia te hale chal riha c). ...
